SAPWIN-MR

Composition: Etoricoxib 60 mg + Thiocolchicoside 4 mg Tablets

Etoricoxib

  • COX-2 inhibitor: Selectively blocks COX-2, reducing inflammation and pain.
  • Pain relief: Used for osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and ankylosing spondylitis.
  • Dosage: Taken once daily.

Thiocolchicoside

  • Muscle relaxant: Centrally acting to relieve spasms.
  • Pain relief: Useful in acute muscle pain when combined with other meds.
  • Dosage: Varies based on condition.

Combination Therapy

  • Combines anti-inflammatory and muscle relaxant effects for:
    • Musculoskeletal pain
    • Back pain
    • Osteoarthritis

Benefits

  • Effective pain and inflammation relief.
  • Reduces muscle spasms.
  • Improves mobility and quality of life.

Side Effects

  • Etoricoxib: GI issues, cardiovascular risk, renal effects.
  • Thiocolchicoside: Drowsiness, dizziness, GI disturbances.

Precautions

  • Consult your doctor before use, especially if you have underlying health conditions.
  • Report side effects promptly.
