SAPWIN-MR

Composition: Etoricoxib 60 mg + Thiocolchicoside 4 mg Tablets

Etoricoxib

  • COX-2 inhibitor: Reduces inflammation and pain.
  • Pain relief: Effective for arthritis, spondylitis, and more.
  • Dosage: Typically once daily.

Thiocolchicoside

  • Muscle relaxant: Relieves muscle spasms.
  • Pain relief: Useful for acute pain with spasms.
  • Dosage: Varies per patient and condition.

Combination Therapy

  • Provides both anti-inflammatory and muscle relaxant effects.
  • Useful for musculoskeletal pain, back pain, osteoarthritis.

Benefits

  • Effective pain and inflammation relief.
  • Improves mobility and quality of life.

Side Effects

  • Etoricoxib: GI issues, cardiovascular risks, kidney effects.
  • Thiocolchicoside: Drowsiness, dizziness, digestive issues.

Precautions

  • Consult a doctor if you have other medical conditions or medications.
  • Report side effects to your healthcare provider immediately.
